Evolving Gameworld

I'm learning how to design browser-based games from the ground up. For added motivation, I decided to build everything here in public. What you see is the game so far.

You can follow my development on Github.

Basic Controls:

  • [A, Left]: Move left
  • [D, Right]: Move right
  • [W, Up, Space]: Jump
  • [S, Down]: Stop movement
  • [R]: Run
That's all for now. Come back next week to see what's new!
